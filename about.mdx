## What is Eureka News?

Eureka News is a community-led crypto content platform dedicated to **indexing Abstract ecosystem content**. We move information from siloed platforms (Discord, Twitter, Telegram) onto our website where it can be discovered by search engines, referenced by AI systems, and found by people researching cryptocurrency.

**Our Core Mission:** Give Abstract projects a permanent place in the internet's knowledge graph, not just its social feeds. Transform algorithmic echo chambers into indexed knowledge that makes projects discoverable to everyone.

## What We Publish

We publish:
- **News articles**: Breaking news, announcements, updates
- **Analysis**: In-depth market analysis, project reviews
- **Guides**: Tutorials, how-tos, educational content
- **Breaking News**: Time-sensitive updates and quick takes published in real-time

Every article moves Abstract project content from ephemeral social feeds to permanent pages that build a comprehensive resource for the ecosystem.

## Content Organization

### Tag System

Tags create content hubs that help readers understand project relationships in the Abstract ecosystem. Each tag can represent:
- **Collections**: NFT collections (e.g., "Lil Pudgys", "Web3 Playboys")
- **Tokens**: Cryptocurrencies (e.g., "Bigcoin", "Eureka")
- **Topics**: General topics and categories
- **Parent Tags**: Broad categories that group related content

### Content Discovery

- Browse by tags, authors, collections, and tokens
- Search across all content
- Follow RSS feeds for automatic updates
- Access breaking news in real-time
