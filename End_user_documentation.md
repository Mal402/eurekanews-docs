## Your First Article

### Creating a New Article

1. **Navigate to Articles**
   - Go to `/admin/articles` in the admin dashboard
   - Click the **"+ New Article"** button

2. **Fill Out the Article Form**

#### Required Fields

**Title**
- Your article headline
- Should be clear and engaging
- Example: "How to Use DeFi Protocols on Abstract Chain"

**Slug**
- Auto-generated from your title
- Used in the article URL
- Can be edited if needed
- Example: `how-to-use-defi-protocols-on-abstract-chain`

**Excerpt**
- A short summary (1-2 sentences)
- Appears in article previews and social media shares
- Should entice readers to click
- Example: "Learn how to interact with DeFi protocols on Abstract Chain, from connecting your wallet to making your first swap."

**Content**
- Your full article content
- Supports **Markdown formatting**
- Can include images, links, code blocks, and more
- See [Content Formatting](#content-formatting) below

**Author**
- Automatically set to you
- Cannot be changed (writers can only publish under their own name)

**Tags** (Required)
- Select 1-3 relevant tags
- You can only select tags you've been assigned
- See [Tag System](#tag-system--permissions) for more details

#### Optional Fields

**Featured Image**
- Click "Choose from Media Library" to select an image
- Or upload a new image
- Recommended size: 1200x630px for best social media display
- Required if you want your article featured on the homepage

**SEO Title** (Optional)
- Custom title for search engines (defaults to your article title)
- Keep it under 60 characters

**SEO Description** (Optional)
- Custom description for search engines (defaults to your excerpt)
- Keep it under 160 characters

**Content Type**
- Choose: `news`, `guide`, or `analysis`
- Affects URL structure
- Example: `/news/article-slug` vs `/guides/article-slug`

**Read Time**
- Estimated reading time in minutes
- Auto-calculated based on word count
- Can be manually adjusted

**Podcast Embed URL**
- If your article has an accompanying podcast episode
- Paste the PodBean embed URL

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**"You don't have permission to use this tag"**
- **Solution**: Request access to the tag from your profile or the article editor
- Tags must be assigned by an editor before you can use them

**"Cannot submit article - missing required fields"**
- **Solution**: Check that all required fields are filled:
  - Title
  - Slug
  - Excerpt
  - Content
  - Tags (at least one)
  - Author

**"Article is read-only"**
- **Reason**: Article is submitted, in review, or published
- **Solution**: 
  - If submitted: Wait for review or withdraw submission
  - If needs revision: Make changes and resubmit
  - If published: Contact an editor for changes

**"Can't find the tag I need"**
- **Solution**: 
  - Request the tag to be created (contact an editor)
  - Or use the closest available tag
  - Check if the tag exists under a different name

**"My application is still pending"**
- **Solution**: 
  - Applications are reviewed manually by editors
  - Wait for email notification
  - Check your application status in the admin dashboard
  - Contact support if it's been more than a week

## FAQ

### How long does article review take?

Review time varies, but typically:
- **First review**: 1-3 business days
- **Revision review**: 1-2 business days
- **Peak times**: May take longer during busy periods

### Can I edit my article after it's published?

No. Published articles are read-only to maintain editorial integrity. If you need changes:
- Contact the editor who reviewed your article
- Or reach out to admin@eurekanews.xyz

### How many tags can I request?

There's no limit, but:
- Request tags you actually plan to use
- Editors review each request
- Too many requests may slow down approval

### Can I write about topics outside my assigned tags?

No. You can only use tags you've been assigned. This ensures:
- Quality control
- Proper topic expertise
- Editorial oversight

### What if my article is rejected?

Rejected articles include feedback from editors. You can:
1. Read the feedback carefully
2. Revise your article based on feedback
3. Resubmit for review
4. Ask questions if feedback is unclear

### Can I delete my article?

Writers cannot delete articles. If you need an article removed:
- Contact an editor
- Explain why removal is needed
- Editors will handle the deletion

### How do I see my article stats?

- View your published articles on `/authors/[your-name]`
- Check clap counts and engagement
- View read counts (if available)

### Can I schedule articles for later?

Not currently. You can:
- Save as draft
- Submit when ready
- Editors publish immediately after approval

### What's the difference between "news", "guide", and "analysis"?

- **News**: Breaking news, announcements, updates
- **Guide**: How-tos, tutorials, educational content
- **Analysis**: In-depth market analysis, project reviews

This affects URL structure and helps readers find content.

### Do I get paid for writing?

Contact the editorial team for information about writer compensation and payment terms.

### Can I write under a pseudonym?

Yes, but:
- Your Twitter account must be verified
- Display name can be different from your Twitter handle
- Contact editors if you want to use a pen name

### How do I improve my chances of getting published?

- **Follow guidelines**: Read and follow this guide
- **Quality over quantity**: Focus on well-researched, valuable content
- **Engage with feedback**: Take revision requests seriously
- **Be consistent**: Regular submissions show commitment
- **Request relevant tags**: Write about topics you're knowledgeable about
- **Remember the mission**: Transform siloed platform information (Discord, Twitter, Telegram) into comprehensive, permanent articles

### What if I need to take a break from writing?

No problem! Your account remains active. You can:
- Leave drafts saved
- Return anytime to continue writing
- Contact editors if you need an extended break

## Editor Guide

### Breaking News Publishing

Editors can publish breaking news for time-sensitive updates that need to go live quickly.

#### Accessing Breaking News

1. **Navigate to Breaking News**
   - Log in to the admin panel
   - In the sidebar, under **Editor Tools**, click **Breaking News**
   - Click **"+ New Breaking News"** to create a post

2. **Quick Publishing Workflow**
   - Breaking news can be published in under 60 seconds
   - Posts automatically go to website, Telegram, and Discord
   - Twitter share opens automatically for manual posting

#### When to Use Breaking News

**Use breaking news for:**
- ‚úÖ Immediate updates: Price movements, announcements, launches
- ‚úÖ Quick takes: Brief analysis or commentary
- ‚úÖ Curated tweets: Share important tweets with context
- ‚úÖ Time-sensitive info: News that can't wait for a full article

**Don't use breaking news for:**
- ‚ùå Long-form content (use regular articles instead)
- ‚ùå In-depth analysis (write a full article)
- ‚ùå Content that needs extensive editing

### Breaking News Best Practices

#### Content Guidelines

**Length & Format:**
- Aim for 50-300 words
- Use line breaks for readability
- Keep it scannable and concise
- Can include Twitter embeds (just paste the URL)

**Tone:**
- Professional but conversational
- Excited for good news, factual for neutral news
- No clickbait or misleading headlines
- Add context to important announcements

**Sources:**
- Link to official announcements
- Embed official tweets when possible
- Cite sources for price data
- Verify information before publishing

#### Using Twitter Embeds

**Best Practice:**
```markdown
Big news from @AbstractChain:

https://twitter.com/AbstractChain/status/1234567890

This changes everything for DeFi on Abstract!
```

**Why this works:**
- Has context before the tweet
- Uses emojis for attention (sparingly)
- Adds commentary after the embed
- Makes the tweet part of a narrative

#### Image Best Practices

**Good Image Types:**
- Screenshots of important tweets ‚úÖ
- Charts and graphs showing data ‚úÖ
- Official announcement graphics ‚úÖ
- Memes (use sparingly and only when appropriate) ü§î

**Image Quality:**
- Minimum 800px wide
- Not blurry or pixelated
- Properly cropped
- Mobile-friendly (no tiny text)
- Recommended: 1200x630px for best social media display

#### Tag Strategy

**Choose relevant, specific tags:**
- ‚úÖ "Abstract Chain", "Lil Pudgys", "DeFi"
- ‚ùå "News", "Update", "Announcement" (too generic)

**Use 1-3 tags:**
- 1 tag: Very focused news (e.g., specific NFT collection)
- 2-4 tags: Broader ecosystem news (e.g., "Abstract" + "DeFi" + "Launch")

#### Publishing Workflow

**Before Publishing:**
1. ‚úÖ Verify the information is accurate
2. ‚úÖ Check spelling and grammar
3. ‚úÖ Ensure tags are relevant
4. ‚úÖ Add featured image if available
5. ‚úÖ Review content for clarity

**During Publishing:**
1. Click **"Publish & Share"**
2. Select channels (Telegram/Discord/Twitter)
3. Review the Twitter compose window
4. Make any final edits to the tweet
5. Post to Twitter manually

**After Publishing:**
- Post appears on website immediately
- Telegram and Discord receive instant updates
- Monitor for any corrections needed
- Edit published posts if typos are found (won't re-post to social)

#### Speed vs. Accuracy

**Priority Order:**
1. **Accuracy first**: Never publish unverified information
2. **Speed second**: Breaking news should be timely, but not at the cost of accuracy
3. **Context matters**: Add brief context even in time-sensitive situations

**When to Wait:**
- If information is unconfirmed
- If you need to verify sources
- If the announcement is unclear
- If you're unsure about facts

**When to Publish Fast:**
- Official announcements from verified sources
