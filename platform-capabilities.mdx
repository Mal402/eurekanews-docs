## What Makes Eureka News Different

Eureka News is built to make Abstract ecosystem content discoverable and permanent. Here are the key capabilities that set us apart:

## Content Discovery

- **Browse by tags** - Navigate content organized by topics, collections, and tokens
- **Collection and token pages** - Each asset has its own page with market data and all related articles
- **Author pages** - Every writer has a dedicated page with their published work
- **Related content** - Discover connected articles through tags and topics

## Content Types

- **Articles**: News, guides, and analysis with full markdown support
- **Breaking News**: Real-time updates published instantly
- **Podcasts**: Embedded audio content in articles
- **Collections & Tokens**: Dedicated pages with market data

## Engagement Features

- **Claps and reactions** to show appreciation
- **Social sharing** to Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn
- **RSS feeds** - subscribe to all articles, breaking news, or tag-specific feeds for automatic updates
- **Newsletter** signup for email updates

## Multi-Channel Distribution

- **Website**: Indexed, searchable content
- **Telegram**: Automatic breaking news posts
- **Discord**: Community updates
- **Twitter**: Social sharing integration
- **RSS**: Syndication to aggregators

## For Content Creators

- **Writer workflow**: Apply, write, submit, and publish
- **Author pages**: Writers automatically get their own author page once they start publishing
- **Tag system**: Request access to write about specific topics
- **Editorial review**: Quality control and feedback
- **Analytics**: Track article performance and engagement
