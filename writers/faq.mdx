## How long does article review take?

Review time varies, but typically:
- **First review**: Typically immediately but up to 1-2 business days
- **Revision review**:  Typically immediately but up to 1-2 business days
- **Peak times**: May take longer during busy periods

## Can I edit my article after it's published?

No. Published articles are read-only to maintain editorial integrity. If you need changes:
- Contact the editor who reviewed your article
- Or reach out to admin@eurekanews.xyz

## How many tags can I request?

There's no limit, but:
- Request tags you actually plan to use
- Editors review each request
- Too many requests may slow down approval

## Can I write about topics outside my assigned tags?

No. You can only use tags you've been assigned. This ensures:
- Quality control
- Proper topic expertise
- Editorial oversight

## What if my article is rejected?

Rejected articles include feedback from editors. You can:
1. Read the feedback carefully
2. Revise your article based on feedback
3. Resubmit for review
4. Ask questions if feedback is unclear

## Can I delete my article?

Writers cannot delete articles. If you need an article removed:
- Contact an editor
- Explain why removal is needed
- Editors will handle the deletion

## How do I see my article stats?

- View your published articles on `/authors/[your-name]`
- Check clap counts and engagement
- View read counts (if available)

## Can I schedule articles for later?

Not currently. You can:
- Save as draft
- Submit when ready
- Editors publish immediately after approval

## What's the difference between "news", "guide", and "analysis"?

- **News**: Breaking news, announcements, updates
- **Guide**: How-tos, tutorials, educational content
- **Analysis**: In-depth market analysis, project reviews

This affects URL structure and helps readers find content.

## Do I get paid for writing?

Contact the editorial team for information about writer compensation and payment terms.

## Can I write under a pseudonym?

Yes, but:
- Your Twitter account must be verified
- Display name can be different from your Twitter handle
- Contact editors if you want to use a pen name

## How do I improve my chances of getting published?

- **Follow guidelines**: Read and follow this guide
- **Quality over quantity**: Focus on well-researched, valuable content
- **Engage with feedback**: Take revision requests seriously
- **Be consistent**: Regular submissions show commitment
- **Request relevant tags**: Write about topics you're knowledgeable about
- **Remember the mission**: Transform siloed platform information (Discord, Twitter, Telegram) into comprehensive, permanent articles

## What if I need to take a break from writing?

No problem! Your account remains active. You can:
- Leave drafts saved
- Return anytime to continue writing
- Contact editors if you need an extended break
