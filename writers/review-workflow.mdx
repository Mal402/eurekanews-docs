## Submitting Your Article

### Complete Your Article

1. Write your full article
2. Add a featured image
3. Select relevant tags
4. Proofread for errors
5. Save Draft

### Click "Submit for Review"

1. A confirmation dialog will appear
2. Confirm to submit
3. Your article status changes to "Submitted"

### What Happens Next

- Article becomes read-only for you
- Editors are notified of your submission
- An editor with matching tag permissions will review it

## During Review

### You'll See

- Status badge showing "Submitted" or "In Review"
- A banner indicating your article is under review
- Read-only view of your article

### You Can

- View your article (but not edit)
- Withdraw your submission (if needed)
- Wait for editor feedback

## Handling Revisions

If an editor requests revisions:

### Read the Feedback

1. Editor feedback appears at the top of your article and emailed to you
2. Review all comments carefully

### Make Changes

1. Article becomes editable again
2. Update based on feedback
3. Save your changes

### Resubmit

1. Click "Resubmit for Review"
2. Your revision history is tracked
3. Editor reviews your updated article
