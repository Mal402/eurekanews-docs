## Breaking News Publishing

Editors can publish breaking news for time-sensitive updates that need to go live quickly.

### Accessing Breaking News

1. **Navigate to Breaking News**
   - Log in to the admin panel
   - In the sidebar, under **Editor Tools**, click **Breaking News**
   - Click **"+ New Breaking News"** to create a post
2. **Quick Publishing Workflow**
   - Breaking news can be published in under 60 seconds
   - Posts automatically go to website, Telegram, and Discord
   - Twitter share opens automatically for manual posting

## Breaking News Best Practices

### Content Guidelines

**Length & Format:**

- Aim for 50-300 words
- Use line breaks for readability
- Keep it scannable and concise
- Can include Twitter embeds (just paste the URL)

**Tone:**

- Professional but conversational
- Excited for good news, factual for neutral news
- No clickbait or misleading headlines
- Add context to important announcements

**Sources:**

- Link to official announcements
- Embed official tweets when possible
- Cite sources for price data
- Verify information before publishing

### Using Twitter Embeds

**Best Practice:**

```markdown
Big news from @AbstractChain:

https://twitter.com/AbstractChain/status/1234567890

This changes everything for DeFi on Abstract!
```

**Why this works:**

- Has context before the tweet
- Uses emojis for attention (sparingly)
- Adds commentary after the embed
- Makes the tweet part of a narrative

### Image Best Practices

**Good Image Types:**

- Screenshots of important tweets ‚úÖ
- Charts and graphs showing data ‚úÖ
- Official announcement graphics ‚úÖ
- Memes (use sparingly and only when appropriate) ü§î

**Image Quality:**

- Minimum 800px wide
- Not blurry or pixelated
- Properly cropped
- Mobile-friendly (no tiny text)
- Recommended: 1200x630px for best social media display

### Tag Strategy

**Choose relevant, specific tags:**

- ‚úÖ "Abstract Chain", "Lil Pudgys", "DeFi"
- ‚ùå "News", "Update", "Announcement" (too generic)

**Use 2-6 tags:**

- 1 tag: Very focused news (e.g., specific NFT collection)
- 2-4 tags: Broader ecosystem news (e.g., "Abstract" + "DeFi" + "Launch")

## Publishing Workflow

### Before Publishing

1. ‚úÖ Verify the information is accurate
2. ‚úÖ Check spelling and grammar
3. ‚úÖ Ensure tags are relevant
4. ‚úÖ Add featured image if available
5. ‚úÖ Review content for clarity

### During Publishing

1. Click **"Publish & Share"**
2. Select channels (Telegram/Discord/Twitter)
3. Review the Twitter compose window
4. Make any final edits to the tweet
5. Post to Twitter manually

### After Publishing

- Post appears on website immediately
- Telegram and Discord receive instant updates
- Monitor for any corrections needed
- Edit published posts if typos are found (won't re-post to social)

## Speed vs. Accuracy

### Priority Order

1. **Accuracy first**: Never publish unverified information
2. **Speed second**: Breaking news should be timely, but not at the cost of accuracy
3. **Context matters**: Add brief context even in time-sensitive situations

### When to Wait

- If information is unconfirmed
- If you need to verify sources
- If the announcement is unclear
- If you're unsure about facts

### When to Publish Fast

- Official announcements from verified sources
- Price movements from reliable data sources
- Confirmed launches and updates
- Curated tweets from official accounts

## Handling Corrections

### If you make a mistake:

1. Edit the post immediately on the website
2. For Telegram/Discord: Post a correction message
3. If major error: Consider deleting and reposting
4. Learn from the mistake for future posts

### Best Practice

- Double-check before publishing
- Have a second pair of eyes for major announcements
- Use draft mode to review before publishing
- Keep a corrections log for learning

## Mobile Publishing

Breaking news can be published from mobile devices:

### Mobile Workflow

1. Open `/admin/breaking-news/new` on your phone
2. Write content using mobile keyboard
3. Paste tweet URLs (they'll auto-embed)
4. Select tags using mobile-friendly checkboxes
5. Upload images from your camera roll
6. Hit Publish - done in 60 seconds!

### Mobile Tips

- Use your phone's Twitter app to copy tweet URLs
- Take screenshots and upload from camera roll
- Voice-to-text works great for quick posts
- Bookmark the breaking news page on your phone

## Editor Checklist

Before publishing breaking news, verify:

- Information is accurate and verified
- Content is clear and concise
- Tags are relevant (2-6 tags)
- Featured image is appropriate (if used)
- Links work correctly
- No typos or grammatical errors
- Context is provided for complex topics
- Sources are cited when needed
