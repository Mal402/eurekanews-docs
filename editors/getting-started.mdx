## Editor Role Overview

Editors are trusted members of the Eureka News community who help maintain content quality while enabling fast publication of breaking news when needed.

### What Editors Can Do

**Editors have all the capabilities of writers, plus additional responsibilities:**

As an editor, you can do everything a writer can do:
- ✅ Create and submit articles for review
- ✅ Request access to tags/topics
- ✅ Manage your profile and author page
- ✅ View your published articles and stats

**Plus, editors have additional capabilities:**

- **Breaking News Publishing**: Publish time-sensitive updates that go live immediately
- **Article Review**: Review, approve, request revisions, or reject writer submissions
- **Tag Management**: Assign tags to writers and manage tag permissions
- **Content Curation**: Help organize and prioritize content across the platform

## Editor Responsibilities

### Breaking News Publishing

Editors can publish breaking news for time-sensitive updates that need to go live quickly. This is one of the key differentiators of the editor role.

### Article Review

Editors review writer submissions to ensure:
- Content quality and accuracy
- Proper formatting and structure
- Appropriate tag usage
- Adherence to editorial guidelines

You can:
- Approve articles for publication
- Request revisions with feedback
- Reject articles that don't meet standards

### Tag Management

Editors help manage the tag system by:
- Assigning tags to writers who request access
- Ensuring tags are used appropriately
- Maintaining tag quality and relevance

### Content Curation

Editors help organize and prioritize content by:
- Reviewing submissions in a timely manner
- Ensuring content diversity across topics
- Maintaining editorial standards
- Coordinating with writers on improvements

## Editor Workflow

### Daily Tasks

1. **Review Submissions**: Check for new articles awaiting review
2. **Publish Breaking News**: When time-sensitive updates occur
3. **Manage Tags**: Review and approve tag access requests
4. **Curate Content**: Ensure quality and diversity of published content

### Best Practices

- **Timely Reviews**: Respond to writer submissions within 1-2 business days
- **Constructive Feedback**: Provide clear, actionable feedback when requesting revisions
- **Consistency**: Apply editorial standards consistently across all submissions
- **Communication**: Maintain open communication with writers

If you're a new editor:

1. **Familiarize yourself with writer capabilities** - Since you have all writer permissions, review the [Writer Documentation](/writers/getting-started)
