## Homepage (`/`)

- **Breaking News Widget**: Latest time-sensitive updates
- **Tag-Based Carousels**: Content organized by topics and tags
- **Most Loved Articles**: Popular articles based on reader engagement
- **Latest Articles**: Most recent publications
- **Price Ticker**: Real-time crypto prices in the navigation bar

## Breaking News (`/breaking`)

- Real-time updates and quick takes
- Filter by tags/topics
- View individual breaking news posts
- Perfect for staying on top of fast-moving developments

## Collections (`/collections`)

- Browse NFT collections on Abstract
- View floor prices, volume, and market data
- See articles and breaking news about each collection
- Access collection social links and marketplaces
- Cast market sentiment vote for each collection

## Coin Directory (`/coins`)

- Browse tokens and cryptocurrencies
- View price charts and trading data
- Read articles and analysis about each token
- Access trading links and DEX information
- Cast market sentiment vote for each token

## Articles (`/articles`)

- Browse all published articles
- Filter by content type: News, Guides, or Analysis
- Sort by date, popularity, or relevance
- Search for specific topics

## Tags (`/tags`)

- Explore content by topic
- Hierarchical tag structure (parent/child tags)
- See article counts and related tags
- Navigate to tag-specific pages for focused content

### Tag Types

Tags can represent:
- **Collections**: NFT collections (e.g., "Lil Pudgys", "Web3 Playboys")
- **Tokens**: Cryptocurrencies (e.g., "Bigcoin", "Eureka")
- **Both**: Some tags are related to both collections and tokens
- **Parent Tags**: Broad categories that group related content (e.g., "Abstract Eco" hosts most tokens and NFTs in the ecosystem)

**Content organization**: Each tag page creates a content hub that helps readers understand project relationships in the Abstract ecosystem.

## Authors (`/authors`)

- Browse all writers on the platform
- View author profiles, bios, and stats
- See all articles and breaking news by each author
- Follow your favorite writers

## Content Types

- **News**: Breaking news, announcements, updates
- **Guides**: How-tos, tutorials, educational content
- **Analysis**: In-depth market analysis, project reviews

<Tip>
Use the search function and filters to quickly find content about specific topics, collections, or tokens you're interested in.
</Tip>
