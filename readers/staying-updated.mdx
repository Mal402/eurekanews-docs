### RSS Setup

1. **Choose an RSS reader** - Popular options include:
   - [Feedly](https://feedly.com) - Web and mobile app
   - [NewsBlur](https://newsblur.com) - Web-based reader
   - [Inoreader](https://www.inoreader.com) - Advanced features
   - Built-in browser readers (Safari, Firefox)

2. **Add the feed URL** - Copy the feed URL from the examples above or visit any tag page to find its feed link

3. **Get automatic updates** - Your reader will check for new content automatically

4. **Integrate into your site** - If you run a website or blog, you can:
   - Display our RSS feed content on your site
   - Use our feeds for content aggregation
   - Link to our articles from your platform
   - All feeds are publicly available and can be embedded or displayed using standard RSS parsers
