## Finding What You Need

### Use Tags Effectively

- Use tags to discover related content
- Explore parent tags to find broader topics
- Check tag pages for comprehensive content hubs
- Tags create content hubs that help you understand project relationships

### Browse by Asset

- Check author pages for writers you trust
- Browse collections/tokens for asset-specific content
- Use breaking news for real-time updates
- **Indexed content** - Unlike Discord/Twitter, all content is searchable

### Search Tips

- Use the search function to find specific topics
- Filter by content type (News, Guides, Analysis)
- Sort by date, popularity, or relevance
- Search works across all articles and breaking news

## Engaging with Content

### Show Appreciation

- Clap on articles you find valuable
- Share content that resonates with you
- Your engagement helps surface quality content

### Follow What Matters

- Subscribe to tags/topics you care about
- Follow authors whose work you enjoy
- Set up RSS feeds for automatic updates

## Staying Informed

### Daily Routine

- Bookmark `/breaking` for quick updates
- Subscribe to RSS feeds for your favorite topics
- Follow us on social media for announcements
- Check the homepage daily for featured content

### Advanced Tips

- Use tag-specific RSS feeds for focused updates
- Follow multiple authors to get diverse perspectives
- Check collection and token pages for asset-specific news
- Use the search function to find historical content

## Privacy & Data

- Your engagement (claps, reactions) is tracked via IP address
- No personal account required to read or interact with content
- Privacy policy information available upon request
- User accounts coming soon for enhanced personalization features

<Tip>
**Pro Tip**: Set up RSS feeds for 3-5 tags you care about most. This gives you a personalized feed without needing to check the site daily.
</Tip>
