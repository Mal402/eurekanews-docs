## Interacting with Content

### Clapping on Articles

- Click the clap button on any article
- Show appreciation for quality content
- Clap counts are visible to everyone
- Engagement is tracked via IP address (no account required)

### Reacting to Breaking News

- Use reaction buttons on breaking news posts
- Quick way to engage with time-sensitive content
- Reactions help surface popular updates

### Sharing Content

- Use share buttons on articles and breaking news
- Share to Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, and more
- Copy article links for easy sharing
- Share buttons appear on every article page

### Newsletter Signup

- Subscribe to receive updates via email
- Newsletter signup forms appear on article pages
- Stay informed about new content and important updates

## Following Authors & Topics

### Author Pages

Visit `/authors/[author-name]` to see:
- All articles by that author
- Breaking news posts
- Author bio and profile
- Writing statistics
- Social media links

### Tag Pages

Visit `/tags/[tag-path]` to see:
- All articles tagged with that topic
- Breaking news related to the tag
- Related tags and subtopics
- Tag description and community info

### Collection & Token Pages

Visit `/collections/[slug]` or `/coins/[symbol]` to see:
- All content about that asset
- Market data and charts
- Social links and marketplaces
- Related articles and breaking news

## Podcast Episodes

- Some articles include embedded podcast episodes
- Listen directly on the article page
- Podcasts provide audio versions of written content
- Look for the podcast player at the top of relevant articles

<Tip>
Engaging with content (clapping, sharing) helps surface quality articles and supports writers. Your engagement matters!
</Tip>
